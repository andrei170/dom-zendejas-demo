Where the math breaks

Selling more time is not the path.

To go from $15M to $30M doing only deals, you would either need to double your team (which is recruiting + management + risk) or double your hours (which is impossible, you are already 7-to-7). Both routes cap out, and neither matches what you said you actually want.

The third route is the product layer your audience is already asking for. Course at $497–997 captures the daily DMs. Cohort upsell at $3–5K is where the take-home actually moves. Neither route adds clients to your day. The course films once, sells forever.

  • Course: 10 days × 1–2 hours of filming, then it runs without you
  • Cohort: 10 spots × $3–5K = $30K–50K per cohort, weekly group calls only
  • Real estate: stays your primary craft, the product layer is built around your existing 7-to-7 calendar

The build

Two pieces. One filming sprint.

Course funnel validates the audience and earns from the daily DMs. The cohort is where the take-home actually moves from $250K to $400K+. Course alone will not get you there. The cohort is the lever. The course is what proves the lever exists.

Piece 1 · Validation

Course funnel at $497–997.

Mid-ticket course, written outline approved by you, filmed in 10 days at 1–2 hours each. Sold off your existing IG audience plus a free lead magnet that captures emails from the daily DMs. The point is to validate that your audience pulls the trigger before you commit time to the higher-leverage cohort.

  • Course outline: drafted by me, approved by you (you fill in the blanks, not write from scratch)
  • VSL script: 5K-word sales letter, read it from a teleprompter
  • Funnel build: opt-in, VSL page, checkout, upsells, all on GoHighLevel
  • Email sequences: abandoned cart, post-purchase, cross-sell to cohort
  • Lead magnet: the answer-to-send for daily DMs, opts them into your list
Math: 10K engaged audience × 0.5% conversion × $697 = ~$35K from one launch. Validates demand. Pays for the build. Sets up the cohort.
Piece 2 · The Lever

Cohort upsell where $250K becomes $400K+.

10-spot beta cohort at $3–5K, opened to course finishers and qualified DMs. Weekly group calls only, no 1-on-1, no DM access, structured so it stays inside your existing 7-to-7. Real scarcity drives the close, real cohort dynamics produce real testimonials, real testimonials sell the next cohort at a higher price.

  • 10 spots, hard cap: close on demand, reopen the next at higher price
  • Format: 8 weeks, 1 weekly group call (60–90 min), shared resource library
  • Application gate: filters out window-shoppers before they hit your inbox
  • No 1-on-1: protects the 7-to-7 calendar by design
Math: 10 spots × $4K = $40K per cohort. Run two per year = $80K. Add $35K course revenue = $115K of new income on top of $250K take-home. That gets you most of the way to your $400K stated goal without selling a single extra house.
